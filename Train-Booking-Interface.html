Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I cancel my ticket?
You can cancel your ticket by going to "My Tickets" section and selecting the ticket you want to cancel. Click on "Cancel Ticket" button and follow the instructions.
What is Tatkal booking?
Tatkal is an emergency quota for passengers who need to travel at short notice. Tatkal booking opens one day before the journey date (excluding the day of journey) at 10:00 AM for AC classes and 11:00 AM for non-AC classes.
How to check PNR status?
You can check your PNR status by going to the "PNR Status" section in the app and entering your 10-digit PNR number.
